## Implementation Checklist
1. Classify observed PIDs as owned/foreign.
2. Default reclaim policy to owned-only; add explicit force option.
3. Implement deterministic signal escalation (`SIGINT`, `SIGTERM`, `SIGKILL`) with waits.
4. Log every reclaim decision with reason and PID list.

## Success Metrics
- Default mode never kills non-owned PIDs.
- Force mode can reclaim foreign PIDs when explicitly enabled.
- Reclaim logs include policy, selected targets, and result.

## Potential Failure Modes And Preventions
- Failure mode: accidental kill of unrelated process.
  Prevention: ownership classifier + force gate.
- Failure mode: reclaim deadlock waiting forever.
  Prevention: bounded waits and fallback escalation.
- Failure mode: no visibility on why launch failed.
  Prevention: include blocking PIDs and policy hint in errors.
