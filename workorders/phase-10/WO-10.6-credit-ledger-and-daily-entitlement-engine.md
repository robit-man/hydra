## Implementation Checklist
1. Add credit account and ledger tables with immutable debit/credit entries.
2. Implement daily entitlement job that computes credit grants from verified asset ownership.
3. Add endpoint to preview entitlement calculation inputs and grant amounts.
4. Add spend reservation/commit flow to prevent double-spend under concurrency.
5. Replace frontend payment node mode selection: `wallet-transfer` and `credit-ledger`.

## Success Metrics
- Eligible users receive daily replenishment credits according to configured entitlement rules.
- Credit debits are atomic, auditable, and idempotent per request id.
- Frontend shows current credit balance and last grant timestamp.
- Services can be gated by minimum credit balance before execution.

## Potential Failure Modes And Preventions
- Failure mode: entitlement grants duplicate on retries.
  Prevention: idempotent grant keys per user/day/rule and unique DB constraints.
- Failure mode: race conditions allow negative balances.
  Prevention: reservation transactions with row-level locking and non-negative checks.
- Failure mode: asset ownership snapshot stale causes wrong grants.
  Prevention: explicit freshness window and forced refresh path before grant run.
- Failure mode: credits and wallet transfers diverge semantically.
  Prevention: explicit mode field per transaction and separate accounting/reporting paths.
