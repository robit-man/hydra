## Potential Failure Modes And Preventions
- Failure mode: nondeterministic timing causes flaky tests.
  Prevention: explicit wait-for-ready + bounded retry windows.
- Failure mode: simulation mutates real user config.
  Prevention: isolate temporary config/runtime directories.
- Failure mode: orphan processes after failure.
  Prevention: `finally` cleanup for all spawned processes.
